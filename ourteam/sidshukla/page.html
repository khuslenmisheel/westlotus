Sid Shukla | RIT | Industrial Design BFA


Sid studied to be a designer, but the job market decided to chop drop his hopes and dreams into oblivion. He now resides in Chicago, Illinois as a substitute teacher. Sid believes that by dealing with 9-year-olds all day, he can better understand grown-up behavior in the workplace.


In truth, he loves teaching and sees it as a critically important responsibility, but that's not as funny.
